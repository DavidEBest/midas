Federal Employee

To access Open Opportunities, you need to create a USAJOBS profile. Make sure you:

  • Choose Federal Employee as your hiring path.
  • Include your current agency in the Experience section.
  • Add your federal email address in the Experience section.
  • Confirm your federal email address — look for the confirmation email in your inbox.

Federal Contractor

If you're a federal contractor and need access to Open Opportunities, you need to create a USAJOBS profile. Make sure you:

  • Choose the hiring path(s) that fit you.
  • Select I am a current contractor working for the Federal Government in the Experience-Federal service section.
  • Include the agency you're currently working for.
  • Add your federal email address.
  • Confirm your federal email address — look for the confirmation email in your inbox.
