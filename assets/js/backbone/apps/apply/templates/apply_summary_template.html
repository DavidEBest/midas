Next steps

We'll walk you through each step of the application process, but here's what you need to know.

Complete your profile

To save time, we'll import your information in your USAJOBS profile into your application. Review your USAJOBS profile to make sure all of your information is correct before you start the application.

If you update your USAJOBS profile after you start the application, you must come back to Open Opportunities and update your application as well.

If you make edits to your application, the information will not update in your USAJOBS profile.

  • 1

    Select internship opportunities

    You must select three internship opportunities to apply to. You can save internship opportunities to apply to later by clicking Save. Find them again in your profile.

  • 2

    Review your experience

    Tell us about your work, military or other experience.

  • 3

    Review your education and transcript

    You'll need to attach your most recent academic transcript. An unofficial transcript is fine.

  • 4

    Review your languages and skills

    Make sure to include any languages you speak, read or write. Also, list your relevant skills.

  • 5

    Include a statement of interest

    Tell us why you're seeking an internship.

  • 6

    Review and submit application

    Double check your application and submit it before 11:59 p.m. ET on the closing date. Don't forget to click Submit.
