Congratulations! Your application has been submitted.

Thanks for submitting your application to the U.S. Department of State Student Internship Program (Unpaid).

What happens next?

The U.S. Department of State will:

  1. Review all applications after the application period closes.
  2. Set up interviews - you'll get an email if you're selected for an interview.
  3. Send offer emails - you'll get another email if you're selected for an internship.

Can I update my application?

Yes, you can update your application by visiting your dashboard. You must make all updates before 11:59 p.m. ET on the closing date. Don't forget to click submit.

See my applications.

  • The U.S. Department of State will review your application to make sure you're eligible and meet the qualifications for the internship. If you meet the qualifications, a hiring manager may email you to set up an interview. Once they finish all interviews, they will let you know if you got the internship.

  • If you're selected for an internship, you must receive either a Secret or Top Secret security clearance. This process may take many months and will require you to complete:

    • An electronic security questionnaire.
    • Fingerprint cards.
    • An in-person interview with an investigator.

    We recommend you don't make any travel or housing plans until you receive your security clearance. Read our FAQs about the security clearance process.

You now have a USAJOBS profile.

With a USAJOBS profile, you can apply to jobs, upload a resume and make it searchable, save job searches and sign up for email notifications.

  • USAJOBS Search

  • Making your resume searchable adds it to the USAJOBS Resume Mining collection. HR specialists and hiring managers from federal agencies use resume mining to look for people (with a searchable resume in their USAJOBS profile) to fill their job vacancies.

    Plus, when your resume is searchable and you have completed your profile, your profile is searchable too. https://www.usajobs.gov/Help/how-to/account/profile/searchable/

  • You can save a search to help you look for jobs in your area of interest. When you save a search, we'll automatically look for jobs that match the keywords and other criteria in your search.
